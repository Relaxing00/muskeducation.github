Web Development

Course Overview

This web development course provides a comprehensive introduction to modern web technologies. Students will learn HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and other essential tools and frameworks to build dynamic and responsive websites.

Duration

1 Years (full-time)

Entry Requirements
  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Basic computer literacy
  • No prior programming experience required
English Language Requirements

Proficiency in English is required to participate in this course. Applicants whose first language is not English must demonstrate English language proficiency by providing one of the following:

  • TOEFL score of 80 or higher
  • IELTS score of 6.0 or higher
  • Other equivalent English language proficiency test results
